Plane Motion of System of Rigid Bodies

Draw FBD with all rigid bodies in the system. Omit forces between each body since it is internal to the system. 1. < 3 unknowns - Use \(\sum F_x, \sum F_y, \sum M\) 2. > 3 unknowns - Seperate FBD for each body. Include internal equal and opposite forces.
