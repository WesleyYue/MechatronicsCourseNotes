Direct Central Impact

Conservation of Momentum \[m_A v_A + m_B v_B = m_A v_A' + m_B v_B'\] Coefficient of Restitution \[v_B' - v_A' = e(v_A - v_B)\]

Oblique Central Impact

\[(v_A)_t = (v_A')_t \quad (v_B)_t = (v_B')_t\] \[m_A (v_A)_n + m_B(v_B)_n = m_A(v_A')_n + m_B(v_B')_n\] \[(v_B')_n - (v_A')_n = e[(v_A)_n - (v_B)_n]\]
