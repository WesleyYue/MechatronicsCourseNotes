## Chapter 15

> ### Rotation of a rigid body about a fixed axis
> 
> #### Uniform Rotation
> 
> $$\theta = \theta_0 + \omega t$$
>
>
> #### Uniform Accelerated Rotation
> 
> $$\omega = \omega_0 + \alpha t$$
> $$\theta = \theta_0 + \omega_0t + \frac{1}{2}\alpha t^2$$
> $$\omega^2 = \omega_0^2 + 2\alpha(\theta - \theta_0)$$
