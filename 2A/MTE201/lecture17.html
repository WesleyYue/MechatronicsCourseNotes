Lecture 17 - Measuring Temperature

A measurement system consists of

st=>start: Measurand
sensor=>operation: Sensor
op2=>operation: Signal Modification System
op3=>operation: Indicator or Recorder
e=>end: Value
st->sensor->op2->op3->e

ex. thermometer

Sensor: thermal expansion/contraction of liquid as function of temp. Signal Modification System: Small diameter tube connected to fluid reservoir Indicator: Scale on tube
